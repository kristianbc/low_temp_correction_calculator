Formula (ICAO Doc 8168):
Correction = H × (15 - t₀) / (273 + t₀ - 0.5 × L₀ × (H + Hₐₛₛ))
Where: H = height above aerodrome, t₀ = aerodrome temperature, L₀ = 0.00198°C/ft, Hₐₛₛ = aerodrome elevation
NOTE: This calculator uses the ICAO Doc 8168 formula for cold temperature corrections. Always follow your airline's standard operating procedures and current documentation.
